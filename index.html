Our Mission

Edutainment that moves people from awareness to action.

Headquartered in Kibera, we blend education with entertainment — using Theatre for Development to spark conversations on peace, gender equity, health, and human rights. Theatre is powerful because it speaks to both the heart and the mind.

Learn more about us
What We Do

Five pillars, one purpose

Peace Building

Performances and workshops that resolve conflict and build unity across tribal lines.

Human Rights

Accessible advocacy on governance, GBV, police brutality, and child rights.

Mentorship

Equipping adolescents with life skills, resilience, and academic support.

Health & SRHR

Reducing maternal mortality and dispelling myths through interactive theatre.

Theatre for Development

Forum theatre that gives voice to the unheard and makes the invisible visible.

Talent & Culture

Platforms for youth to showcase talent and celebrate cultural pride.
