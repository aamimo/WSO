Achievements

A decade of impact, on stage and beyond.

Through partnerships with NGOs and government institutions, Wasanii Sanaa has helped shape conversations and outcomes across Kenya and beyond.

Participated in the 2025 XVIII Festival Sztuki Ludowej in Poland and Sannio MondialFestivals in Italy.

Created awareness on peaceful coexistence in collaboration with Life & Peace Institute and Mercy Corps Kenya.

Empowered communities on the reduction of gender-based violence with Gender Violence Recovery Centre and Women Empowerment Link.

Conducted joint dialogues and interactive community theatre raising awareness on corruption with Transparency International Kenya.

Created human rights education awareness in schools and communities in partnership with Amnesty International Kenya.

Reduced stigma and discrimination toward people living with HIV/AIDS through community interactive theatre with the National AIDS Control Council.

Participated in the International Nairobi Trade Fair from 2014 – 2025.

2018 Gender-Thon winners — organized by the Centre for Rights Education and Awareness (CREAW).
