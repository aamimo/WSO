About Us

Empowering youth, transforming communities.

Wasanii Sanaa is a community-based youth organization founded in 2013 and registered with the Kenyan Ministry of Gender, Sports, Culture and Social Services.

Headquartered in the heart of Kibera informal settlement, Wasanii Sanaa operates as a grassroots initiative empowering marginalized communities through the power of performing arts. As a self-organized group, we focus on addressing local challenges like poverty, gender inequality, and human rights violations by blending entertainment with education.

We use edutainment — a fusion of education and entertainment — as a tool for advocacy and capacity building. Our work emphasizes Theatre for Development, which involves interactive performances that spark community dialogue.

Motto

Art for Change, Voice for All.

Vision

A vibrant, equitable society where every youth voice shapes a just future through the transformative power of art.

Mission

To empower youth and marginalized communities to drive social justice, human rights, and sustainable change through innovative, art-based education and advocacy.

Objectives

What we set out to achieve

  • 1

    Create a youth-friendly platform for active participation in community building.

  • 2

    Nurture and improve youth talent through skills development.

  • 3

    Drive income generating activities and creation of employment.

  • 4

    Build networks that support youthful programs.
