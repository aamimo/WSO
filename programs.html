Programs

Five focus areas, one shared purpose.

Each program blends performance, education and grassroots organizing to address the real challenges facing our communities.

01

Peace Building Initiative

We use art to resolve community conflicts and promote unity. Performances and workshops encourage dialogue across tribal and clan lines, contributing to peace networks in society.

02

Human Rights Education

In collaboration with like-minded organizations we tackle governance, poverty, drug addiction, early child marriages, police brutality, and gender-based violence — making rights advocacy engaging, not lecturing.

03

Mentorship

Programs for adolescent boys and girls focused on academic performance, retention, and life skills. We aim to reduce teenage pregnancies while fostering personal development and resilience.

04

Health (SRHR & MHR)

Community sensitization through Interactive Participatory Educative Theatre, radio talk shows and events — reducing unintended pregnancies and dispelling myths around sexual and maternal health.

05

Theatre for Development

Participatory and Forum Theatre that empowers communities, sparks dialogue on social issues, promotes cultural pride, and gives youth a platform to display their talents.
